# Durable Decision Template

## Title

Short, searchable decision title.

## Status

Use a small status such as:

- proposed
- accepted
- superseded

## Context

What problem, ambiguity, or tradeoff made this decision necessary?

## Decision

What was chosen?

## Why

Why was this choice preferred over the alternatives?

## Alternatives considered

- Option A
- Option B
- Option C

## Consequences

What changes because of this decision?

Include both:

- benefits
- limitations or tradeoffs

## Boundaries

What does this decision **not** decide?

## Related artifacts

Link related materials such as:

- roadmap item
- governance doc
- policy pack
- contract
- pilot note
